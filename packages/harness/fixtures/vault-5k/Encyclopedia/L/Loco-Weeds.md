# Loco-Weeds

LOCO-WEEDS, or CRAZY-WEEDS, leguminous plants, chiefly species of
_Astragalus_ and _Lupinus_, which produce a disease in cattle known as
"loco-disease." The name is apparently taken from the Spanish _loco_,
mad. The disease affects the nervous system of the animals eating the
plants, and is accompanied by exhaustion and wasting.
