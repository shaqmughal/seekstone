# Bird-Louse

BIRD-LOUSE, any small flat degenerate wingless neuropterous insect of
the group Mallophaga, parasitic upon birds and mammals and feeding upon
dermal excretions or upon the softer parts of hair and feathers. The
term "biting-lice" is sometimes given to these parasites, in allusion to
the mandibulate character of their mouth-parts, which serves to
distinguish them at once from the true lice of the order Rhynchota in
which the jaws are haustellate.
