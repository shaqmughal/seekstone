# Biribi

BIRIBI, or CAVAGNOLE, a French game of chance, prohibited by law since
1837. It is played on a board on which the numbers 1 to 70 are marked.
The players put their stakes on the numbers they wish to back. The
banker is provided with a bag from which he draws a case containing a
ticket, the tickets corresponding with the numbers on the board. The
banker calls out the number, and the player who has backed it receives
sixty-four times his stake; the other stakes go to the banker. In the
French army "to be sent to Biribi" is a cant term for being sent to the
disciplinary battalion in Algeria.
