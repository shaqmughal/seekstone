# Bischweiler

BISCHWEILER, a town of Germany, in the imperial territory of
Alsace-Lorraine, district of Lower Alsace, 23 m. by rail N. by E. from
Strassburg. Pop. (1900) 7897. It has manufactures of jute and machinery,
brewing and iron-founding.
