# Huercal Overa

HUERCAL OVERA, a town of south-eastern Spain, in the province of
Almeria, on the Lorca-Baza railway, and between two branches of the
river Almanzora. Pop. (1900) 15,763. Huercal Overa is the chief town of
a thriving agricultural district, largely dependent for its prosperity
on the lead mining carried on among the surrounding highlands.
