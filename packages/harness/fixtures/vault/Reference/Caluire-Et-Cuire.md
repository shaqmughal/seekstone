# Caluire-Et-Cuire

CALUIRE-ET-CUIRE, a town of eastern France, in the department of Rhone,
2½ m. N. by E. of Lyons by rail. Pop. (1906) 9255. It has manufactures
of coarse earthenware and hard-ware, copper and bronze foundries and
nursery-gardens.
