# Invoice

INVOICE (originally a plural, _Invoyes_ or _Invoys_, of _Invoy_, a
variant of "envoy," from the French _envoyer_, to send), a statement
giving full particulars of goods sent or shipped by a trader to a
customer, with the quantity, quality and prices, and the charges upon
them. Consular invoices, i.e. invoices signed at the port of shipment by
a consul of the country to which the goods are being consigned, are
generally demanded by those countries which impose _ad valorem_ duties.
